Who We Are

Houses I Buy is a privately owned real estate investment company operating exclusively in the Dallas–Fort Worth metroplex and its surrounding counties. We are not a franchise, not a national chain, and not a real estate brokerage. We are local investors — people who live and work in North Texas — who purchase homes directly from sellers using our own cash funds.

Since 2010, we have helped hundreds of DFW homeowners in difficult situations move on quickly and confidently. We have bought homes in every condition imaginable, from pristine properties to those with severe foundation damage, fire damage, title complications, and probate entanglements.

Our Core Philosophy

We believe that selling a home should never feel like a punishment. A motivated seller shouldn't have to clean, repair, negotiate with strangers, or wait four months for a bank to approve a buyer's loan. Our job is to remove every single friction point from the process and put cash in your hand as fast as possible — on your timeline, at a fair price.

We Are Real Estate Investors — Not Agents

This distinction matters enormously to you as a seller. A licensed real estate agent's job is to list your home publicly on the MLS, market it to hundreds of potential buyers, schedule open houses and showings, then wait — sometimes 60 to 90 days — for a qualified buyer to appear and for their lender to approve a loan. Their 5%–6% commission comes out of your proceeds at closing.

Credibility & Safety FAQ

Are you a licensed real estate company in Texas?
We are a private real estate investment company, not a licensed brokerage. In Texas, private investors who purchase property with their own funds are not required to hold a real estate license. We are, however, fully registered business entities in the State of Texas and operate under all applicable Texas Property Code regulations.
How do I know your cash offer is real and the funds actually exist?
Every transaction we close goes through an independent, licensed Texas title company — not us directly. The title company verifies that our funds exist and are properly deposited into escrow before you ever sign a single document. You are never at risk of a "fake check" situation because a neutral third party controls the money.
Is my personal information kept private and secure?
Absolutely. We never sell, share, or distribute your contact or property information to third parties, real estate agents, or data brokers. The information you submit is used solely by our internal team to prepare your cash offer and communicate with you about the transaction.
What happens if I accept your offer and then change my mind?
Texas law provides sellers with a right of rescission period. Beyond that, we handle contract cancellations on a case-by-case basis with full transparency. We have never pursued legal action against a seller who changed their mind. Our reputation is built on being trustworthy and pressure-free, and we intend to keep it that way.
How long have you been buying houses in DFW?
We have been active in the DFW real estate market since 2010 — through the post-recession recovery, the explosive growth years of the 2010s, the COVID market surge, and the current interest rate environment. We have seen every market condition this region produces and we are equipped to close deals in any of them.
