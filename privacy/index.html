Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2025

Houses I Buy ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, housesibuy.com, or submit information to request a cash home offer.

1. Information We Collect

When you voluntarily fill out forms on our website, we collect personal details necessary to evaluate your real estate property and make a fair cash offer. This includes:

  • Your Name
  • Property Address
  • Phone Number
  • Email Address
  • Any specific details about your home or situation you choose to share

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data strictly for business operations related to buying your home. We explicitly use it to:

  • Contact you regarding your cash offer inquiry.
  • Analyze comparable sales data and local real estate public records to formulate an offer.
  • Coordinate the closing transaction through a licensed Texas title company if you accept our offer.

3. Strict Third-Party Data Protection

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with third-party data brokers, marketing lists, or unauthorized real estate brokerages. Your details stay secure with our internal investment acquisition team. The only time your information is shared legally is with an independent, licensed Texas title company to handle escrow and closing papers if we enter into an official sales agreement.

4. Data Security

We utilize secure data handling protocols and form processing services (including Formspree) to protect your submissions from unauthorized access or disclosure.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us directly at info@housesibuy.com.
