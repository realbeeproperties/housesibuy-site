Frequently Asked Questions
About Selling for Cash in DFW

Thinking about how to sell your house fast in Dallas TX? Here are honest answers to the questions we hear most often.

How do you calculate your cash offer? Will I get a lowball offer?
We use a transparent formula: Cash Offer = After Repair Value (ARV) − Cost of Repairs − Selling/Holding Costs − Our Minimum Profit. We analyze recent fully renovated sales on your exact street and subtract local repair costs. You get an honest offer reflecting your property’s current as-is condition.
How are you different from a traditional DFW real estate agent?
Agents list your house on the MLS and charge a 5% to 6% commission fee out of your pocket, requiring public showings, repairs, and lengthy bank delays. Houses I Buy is the direct buyer. We do not list your house, we don’t charge fees, and we handle closing costs completely.
Can I sell an inherited house if it hasn’t gone through Texas probate yet?
Yes. If an inherited estate hasn’t gone through formal probate court, we can work directly with local DFW title companies to utilize an Affidavit of Heirship. This allows legal heirs to quickly establish title ownership and sell the property safely without extra taxes or month-long courtroom battles.
Do I need to fix foundation issues, clean up, or make repairs before selling?
No, you don’t have to lift a finger. We buy residential properties in 100% absolute as-is condition. Shifting clay soil causes major foundation cracks across North Texas, which scares away traditional buyers. We absorb those foundation repair costs and cleanouts ourselves. Take what you want, leave the rest behind.
How fast can we close, and is my transaction secure?
We can close in as little as 7 days, or on the exact date that works best for your schedule. To guarantee total protection, all transactions are processed through independent, fully insured local Texas Title Companies. They verify the funds, handle the legal deeds, and wire your cash directly to your bank account.
